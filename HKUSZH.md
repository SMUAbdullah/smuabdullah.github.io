## Guidelines for WeChat booking

### Scan this QR code via WeChat and follow the account

<img src="https://github.com/SMUAbdullah/smuabdullah.github.io/blob/main/images/HKUSZH/818462.png" width="250" height="250">

### Tap on services

<img src="https://github.com/SMUAbdullah/smuabdullah.github.io/blob/main/images/HKUSZH/main.jpeg" width="250" height="500">

- Tap on 普通门诊 (general outpatient clinic) under 医疗服务 (medical services)

<img src="https://github.com/SMUAbdullah/smuabdullah.github.io/blob/main/images/HKUSZH/options.jpg" width="250" height="500">

- Tap on Add Patient
  
<img src="https://github.com/SMUAbdullah/smuabdullah.github.io/blob/main/images/HKUSZH/homepage.JPG" width="250" height="500">

- Fill in your details (Name, Identity Document, Identity Document number, phone number)

<img src="https://github.com/SMUAbdullah/smuabdullah.github.io/blob/main/images/HKUSZH/pat_details.JPG" width="250" height="500">

- Tap on Appointment
<img src="https://github.com/SMUAbdullah/smuabdullah.github.io/blob/main/images/HKUSZH/homepage.JPG" width="250" height="500">

- Choose the department and subdepartment (may need to translate if can't read Chinese)
<img src="https://github.com/SMUAbdullah/smuabdullah.github.io/blob/main/images/HKUSZH/dept.jpg" width="250" height="500">

- Choose an appointment date and time of day
<img src="https://github.com/SMUAbdullah/smuabdullah.github.io/blob/main/images/HKUSZH/options.jpg" width="250" height="500">
![Date and time](https://github.com/SMUAbdullah/smuabdullah.github.io/blob/main/images/HKUSZH/appt.jpeg)

- Choose the exact time slot
<img src="https://github.com/SMUAbdullah/smuabdullah.github.io/blob/main/images/HKUSZH/options.jpg" width="250" height="500">
![Time slot](https://github.com/SMUAbdullah/smuabdullah.github.io/blob/main/images/HKUSZH/time.jpeg)

- Tap on the green box and pay via WeChat
<img src="https://github.com/SMUAbdullah/smuabdullah.github.io/blob/main/images/HKUSZH/options.jpg" width="250" height="500">
![Payment](https://github.com/SMUAbdullah/smuabdullah.github.io/blob/main/images/HKUSZH/payment.jpg)
