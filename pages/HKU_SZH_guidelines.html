General guidelines
  • Morning booking: Avoid evening sessions — if a procedure is needed, equipment may not be available later.
  • At the hospital: Go to outpatient dept, use self-service kiosk to print queue number. Show the printed queue number to the nurse at the reception. Wait for your turn.
  • Payment via WeChat: Ensure Mainland payment is set up. Bring a data SIM for connectivity.
  • Location: Next to Shenzhen Bay Park Metro Station. Transport guide ↗
  • While waiting: Relax inside hospital or stroll to Shenzhen Bay Park (nearby).
